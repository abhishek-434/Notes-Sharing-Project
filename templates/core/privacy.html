Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your username, email address, and optional profile information such as your name, college name, department, and bio. When you upload notes, we store the file and its metadata.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate and improve NotesHub, to notify you about interactions with your content (comments, downloads), and to communicate platform updates.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your profile information (name, college) is visible to other registered users.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session and remember your dark/light mode preference. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at our contact page.
